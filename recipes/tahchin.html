Tahchin

Tahchin is a popular Persian dish also called Persian rice cake. It is made up of two layers of rice that cover a middle meat layer. The most common type of meat for Tahchin is chicken. However, in some regions they use lamb, too. If you are vegan, you can use different kinds of mushrooms as a substitute.

Ingredients

  • rice: 2 cups
  • boneless skinless chicken breast: 500 g (or two large pieces)
  • saffron: 1/8 tsp

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken 12 hours in advance.
  2. Cook the rice until partially soft.
  3. Mix the rice with yogurt and eggs.
  4. Add the layers and cook for 2 hours.
  5. Top with cooked barberries.
